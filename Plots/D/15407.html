Figure Caption: 

Invariant-mass distributions of the $\mathrm{D}^+$ in the interval 5 < $p_{\mathrm{T}}$ < 5.5 GeV/$c$  for the centrality class 0--10\%. Fit values $\mu$ and $\sigma$ are reported as well as the raw yield S.

Detail description: 

Invariant-mass distributions of the $\mathrm{D}^+$ in the interval 5 < $p_{\mathrm{T}}$ < 5.5 GeV/$c$  for the centrality class 0--10\%. Fit values $\mu$ and $\sigma$ are reported as well as the raw yield S.
